My Story

It ain’t always easy finding that perfect gardener. The one that is chatty enough but not annoying, who gets the job done in time and doesn’t leave any mess behind. Sounds fantastic, right?
My name is Stephen Mewies and I have a passion for gardening and plants. I studied horticulture at Brooksby Melton College, and have a NVQ level 3 National Diploma in horticulture. My business was established in 2013 and has grown since then. I hold full public liability insurance and I'm registered on the Age UK business directory.
I am based in Stapleford, Nottingham and work in the local area including: Sandiacre, Beeston, Bramcote, Chillwell and Toton.
Please contact me for a free quotation.
I look forward to hearing from you.

Offered

Services

Lawn care / cutting

Whether the quality and finished look of your lawn is important to you or you simply want a tidy looking lawn Stephen have the answer; passionate about lawns and will not only provide you with a reliable weekly or fortnightly service they will make certain that your requirements are not only met but exceeded.

Hedge cutting

New hedges must be pruned regularly in order to ensure they grow into the desired shape. If this is not done the hedges can grow in an unruly manner. Hedge reduction and pruning services from Elm Tree can ensure your hedges look neat and tidy.
I offer a range of services from cutting new hedges to reducing mature hedges and much more. You can count on me to deliver top quality services at competitive prices.

Small tree pruning

Ornamental garden trees require minimal maintenance, but a little sensible pruning can ensure the tree remains healthy and safe and grows in an attractive shape.
I offer mall tree pruning and general pruning.

Weeding & tidying

In this area, late spring and early summer offers an extremely good growing environment for all plants, including weeds. Weeding is necessary not just to keep a garden looking neat and tidy, but to optimise the growing conditions for the garden plants.
My services include regular hand and mechanical weeding, and weed control by weed-killer.

Planting and planting advice

I have been engaged in providing expert planting advice for many years. The rationale that informs all of my services for planting is to support my clients to achieve a landscape with thriving plants that are excellently suited to the location where they are growing.

General garden advice

Stephen Mewies Gardening Services is dedicated to help you achieve your gardening dreams and projects. I will provide you with gardening advice, Plus your own personal garden expert to help you get started on a gardening project.
Don't hesitate to ask for gardening advice.
